# 🛍️ Amazon Sales Report - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an Amazon sales dataset. The goal is to extract meaningful insights and trends that can support **data-driven business decisions** related to product performance, customer behavior, fulfillment, and regional sales.

---

## 📌 Project Objectives

1. **Sales Overview**  
   Understand overall sales performance and identify key trends and patterns over time.

2. **Product Analysis**  
   Evaluate the distribution of product categories, sizes, and quantities to identify popular items and trends.

3. **Fulfillment Analysis**  
   Assess the effectiveness of different fulfillment methods used in delivering orders.

4. **Customer Segmentation**  
   Segment customers based on buying behavior and location for targeted marketing strategies.

5. **Geographical Analysis**  
   Explore how sales are distributed across different states and cities.

6. **Business Insights**  
   Deliver actionable recommendations to optimize sales strategies, inventory management, and customer service.
